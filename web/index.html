This is the home for our Open Food Data movement. We are working towards an open standard for food information online.

The people involved in this are from all over the world, and believe that while there is an explosion in the number of services created to help people make better choices about how we produce, consume, and interact with food, challenges related to the accuracy and completeness of data hamper the rate of innovation.

By sharing information about and making it easier to communicate about food in our food systems, we hope to enable the many organizations and communities working to improve how we eat.

Open Food Data Standard

The open food data standard aims to be participatory, and to promote a better food system by supporting information sharing to enhance the work of the many people who strive to improve access to healthy local food, promote small farms and food businesses, and many other critical features of a healthy food system.

API & Data

Open Food API
  • An early stage API. Provides a list of Foods with UUID's.
  • There are over 7,000 foods listed.
  • We plan to branch out to share more kinds of data that can be linked to the Foods.
  • If you notice foods are missing, you can help improve the data by becoming a Data Captain.
